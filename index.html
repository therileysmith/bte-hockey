The BTE Difference

Our mission is to constantly strive to develop our athletes' physical and psychological potential for the game of hockey by pushing them beyond the edge of their capabilities to meet their goals and increase their love for the game.

Our Method in Action

We specialize in one-on-one/team training utilizing its own Global Slick synthetic ice rink and fully equipped off-ice training facility. In addition to BTE's facility, we also have access to real ice next door at Hot Shot Ice Arena and Turf Time Field House.
