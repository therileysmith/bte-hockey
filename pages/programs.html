Programs

On-Ice Training

On-Ice Development Evaluation

30 minute session $50

During the evaluation your player's skating and puck skills will be assessed. Based on their strengths and weaknesses, we create a custom program that is designed to enhance their hockey development.

Power Skating

30 minute sessions are available to be purchased in 5, 10, or 20 packs

Power Skating session have an emphasis on stride development and focus on building the player's edge control.

Skills Program

30 minute sessions are available to be purchased in 5, 10, or 20 packs

Our Skills Program is a progressive power skating program that strvies to enhance the player's stick handling, passing/receiving, and shooting skills.

Game Scenario

30 or 45 minute sessions are available to be purchased in 5, 10, or 20 packs

With our Game Scenario training method player's get a chance learn and develop in-game concepts that put their hangling, passing, and shooting techniques to the test with the added pressure of the game.

Which program do I pick?

Prior to any package or program design, each player must go through our evaluation process. The evaluation is held on our synthetic ice where the coach will evaluate the player on their strengths and weaknesses in all areas of their game.

Following the evaluation the coach will determine the best program for your son/daughter.

Please contact us if you have an questions or would like to schedule a session!
